### Privacy Policy

At **FM Dictionary**, we are committed to safeguarding your personal data.

### 1. Login Information
When you use Google or Apple Sign-in, we only store:
* Email address
* Full name
* Profile picture
*This is used solely for account identification and progress synchronization.*

### 2. Audio Data
When utilizing our **AI voice recognition**, your audio is processed solely to be converted into text. We **do not store** these recording files on our servers.

### 3. Our Commitment
We **never sell or share** your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.
